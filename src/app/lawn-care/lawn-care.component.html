Business Overview

At GreenScape, we are dedicated to providing professional lawn care services that go beyond aesthetics. Our mission is to create vibrant, healthy lawns that enhance the beauty of your property while considering environmental sustainability. Trust GreenScape for comprehensive lawn care solutions tailored to your lawn's unique needs.

Empowering homeowners and businesses with top-tier lawn care services, promoting environmental stewardship, and creating green spaces that thrive in all seasons.

Homeowners, property managers, and businesses looking for professional lawn care services to achieve and maintain a lush, healthy lawn.

Services

  • Fertilization and Weed Control
  • Aeration and Overseeding
  • Pest and Disease Management

Our revenue is generated through personalized lawn care packages, one-time services, and premium offerings such as organic fertilization options. Additional revenue streams include partnerships with local nurseries for plant supply and educational workshops.
